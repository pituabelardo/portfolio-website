island 01 · video campaign · liga u

capullos

in spanish, "capullos" means flower buds. it also means little punks. that's the whole campaign.

video case — youtube embed goes here

(waiting for the link, see readme)

young players get doubted for a living. too green, too cocky, too soon. capullos flips the insult: buds are exactly what blooms. the campaign frames liga u's under-22 talent as flowers about to open — and dares you to keep calling them capullos when they do.

liga u — brand & communication strategy

a league nobody had heard of, a voice everybody under 22 recognizes. built from zero.

liga u is spain's u22 national basketball league — a league that didn't exist two seasons ago. building it meant deciding what it sounds like before deciding what it looks like: a brand strategy that treats under-22 players as protagonists (not prospects), an identity that survives a phone screen at full speed, and an online communication engine that publishes like a fan and thinks like a league.

island 03 · print concept · royal talens x pantone

talens x pantone

color is art. not a barrier.

two brands that live off color, going black and white on purpose. the campaign takes real border photography — melilla, lampedusa, chihuahua — and strips the color out, because when color means skin, nobody's admiring the palette. the only color left is the pantone chip: color is art. not a barrier.

concept campaign for royal talens x pantone, developed during my brand design specialization at hogeschool utrecht.

island 04 · awareness concept · ministerie van justitie en veiligheid

ministerie van justitie

un-usual suspects. think before you click.

online scammers don't look like movie hackers. they look like anyone — even like ruth, 82, asking you to hold her bags. the campaign puts grandmas and grandpas in balaclavas to make one point stick: on the internet you never know who's on the other side, so think before you click.

concept awareness campaign for the dutch ministerie van justitie en veiligheid, developed during my brand design specialization at hogeschool utrecht.
